How to cite

If you have used Designs and Groups in the preparation of a paper, preprint, thesis, presentation, software package, or other scholarly work, please cite the database as described below.

Seyed Hassan Alavi and Ashraf Daneshkhah, Designs and Groups: constructions and classifications (database), In preparation. https://designs-groups.github.io/

BibTeX

@misc{ADa,
  author       = {Alavi, Seyed Hassan and Daneshkhah, Ashraf},
  title        = {Designs and Groups: constructions and classifications},
  howpublished = {Database},
  note         = {In preparation},
  url          = {https://designs-groups.github.io/}
}

When a specific design, construction, classification result, or family in the database is based on earlier work, please also cite the corresponding original source, which may be listed in the Bibliography.
