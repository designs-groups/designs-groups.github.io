About the authors

Designs and Groups is developed by Seyed Hassan Alavi and Ashraf Daneshkhah, professors in the Department of Mathematics Faculty of Science, Bu-Ali Sina University. Their research interests lie in finite group theory and algebraic combinatorics, with particular emphasis on permutation groups, automorphism groups, character theory, and highly symmetric combinatorial designs.
